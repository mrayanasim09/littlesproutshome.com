Cookies Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit and can improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

Currently, we do not use cookies on our website for tracking or marketing purposes. We may use cookies in the future for:

  • Essential functionality (remembering your preferences)
  • Analytics (understanding how visitors use our site)
  • Improving user experience

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
