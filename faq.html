Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you accept?

We welcome children from 3 months to 5 years old. Please contact us to discuss your child's individual needs.

What are your hours?

Our hours are Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:30 PM. Reach out to confirm current availability.

How do I enroll?

Start by contacting us to schedule a visit and learn about enrollment options.

Do you provide meals?

Yes. We provide healthy meals and snacks, and we can discuss dietary preferences during your visit.

What should my child bring?

We provide a suggested list based on your child's age and routine. Contact us for guidance.

What is your sick-child policy?

We follow a thoughtful approach to wellness and ask families to keep children home when they are ill to protect everyone.

Is there outdoor play?

Yes. When weather permits, children enjoy structured outdoor time each day.

Do you offer part-time care?

Yes. We offer full-time, part-time, and after-school care based on current openings.
